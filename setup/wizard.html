How to export from Google Search Console
  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
  2. Select your property (domain)
  3. Click Performance in the left sidebar
  4. Set your date range (last 3 months recommended)
  5. Click the Export button (top right) → Download CSV
  6. Unzip the downloaded file — you'll get Chart.csv, Queries.csv, Pages.csv, etc.
